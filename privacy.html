Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 December 2025

Survival Protocol X respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Survival Protocol X does not collect, store, share, or process any personal or non-personal user data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No analytics or tracking is used
  • No location data is accessed
  • No device identifiers are collected
  • No accounts or registrations are required

The application works fully offline and does not communicate with external servers.

2. Permissions

Survival Protocol X does not request sensitive permissions. Any basic permissions required by the Android operating system are used only to ensure proper app functionality and are not used for data collection.

3. Third-Party Services

The application does not use third-party services such as analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting systems. No data is shared with third parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

Survival Protocol X does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is fully compliant with children’s privacy protection regulations.

5. Data Security

Because the application does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data leakage or unauthorized access.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be reflected on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically.
